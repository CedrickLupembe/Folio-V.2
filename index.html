01. About Me

Hello! My name is Cedrick and I enjoy revolutionize the world by creating things on the internet accessible to everyone. My interest in web development started in 2019 when I really decided to understand what happens when I was doing research on the internet.
I started by self-training through youtub tutorials and w3schools learning HTML and CSS these two platforms taught me a lot.

Today I have the privilege of working in a club of talented guys in development with whom we spend a wonderful time. We also give training to beginners. My main goal these days is to create more accessible products for a variety of customers.
